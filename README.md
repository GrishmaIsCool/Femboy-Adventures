# Femboy-Adventures
Puzzle/Adventure indie game made by me :)



Femboy Adventures is a narrative-driven indie game about a streamer named Sam and his girlfriend Emily, who find themselves trapped inside a strange game world after a something happens to Emily in a forest. What starts as a weird accident quickly becomes something much bigger than either of them expected.

Story

Sam and Emily have to navigate a game world full of eccentric NPCs, bad puzzles, and a grumpy tomboy named Rani who hates femboys and steals everyone's snacks. They adventure to confront the witch who turned Emily into a femboy.

Features

Narrative-driven gameplay with dialogue-heavy storytelling.
A cast of characters that will make you laugh and then unexpectedly make you feel things.
Four part story structure with escalating stakes.
Meta-narrative that plays with the idea of games and scripts.
