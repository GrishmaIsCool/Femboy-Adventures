# Femboy-Adventures
Puzzle/Adventure indie game made by me :)



Femboy Adventures is a narrative-driven indie game about a streamer named Sam and his girlfriend Emily, who find themselves trapped inside a strange game world after a deal goes wrong in a forest. What starts as a weird accident quickly becomes something much bigger than either of them expected.

Story

Sam and Emily have to navigate a game world full of eccentric NPCs, bad puzzles, and a grumpy tomboy named Rani who hates femboys and steals everyone's snacks. Somewhere above all of it, developers are watching. And somewhere above the developers, something else is watching too.

Features

Narrative-driven gameplay with dialogue-heavy storytelling
Puzzle mechanics that reward doing things wrong
A cast of characters that will make you laugh and then unexpectedly make you feel things
Four part story structure with escalating stakes
Meta-narrative that plays with the idea of games, scripts, and player agency
16-bit pixel art style

Characters

Sam — Streamer. Protagonist. Not ready for any of this.
Emily — Actress. Femboy. Somehow handling it better than Sam.
Rani — Tomboy. Villain in training. Snack enthusiast.
The Witch — Administrator. Grandmother. Her face is hidden for no reason anyone can explain.
